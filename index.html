Home page of Kaushik Mohanta

I have obtained PhD degree from the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, India on 2022. My thesis supervisors were Prof. Kaushik Bal and Prof. Prosenjit Roy. I am doing research in Analysis of Partial Differential Equations. In particular, my research interests are Fractional Sobolev Spaces, Fractional Poincare and Hardy Inequalities, Fractional Orlicz Sobolev Spaces, Magnetic Fractional Sobolev Spaces.
